Privacy Policy

Domino & Konkan - Score Keeper

Last updated: July 7, 2026

📋 Overview

Domino & Konkan - Score Keeper is a simple score tracker for the games of Domino and Konkan. Your privacy matters to us, and this app was built to respect it completely.

This app collects no personal data whatsoever. There are no accounts, no login, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

🔒 Data We Collect

We collect nothing. To be specific:

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No accounts and no login are required or offered.
  • No analytics are gathered.
  • No tracking of any kind takes place.

📱 Player Names & Scores

Any player names and scores you enter are stored only on your device. This information is never transmitted anywhere, never uploaded to a server, and never shared with anyone.

🎙️ Microphone & Audio Permission

The microphone and audio permission is used only to play sounds and spoken score announcements during your game.

No audio is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted. The permission exists solely so the app can produce sound.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Safe for Everyone

  • Safe for all ages.
  • Contains no ads.
  • Uses no third-party services.
